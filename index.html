About

Data Analyst well-versed in SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI

I bring a unique perspective to data analysis, having a degree in Behavioral Neuroscience and transitioning from the world of healthcare operations. My experience in Revenue Cycle Management and Clinical Care Management provided me with a deep understanding of the healthcare landscape and its data challenges.

Eager to leverage data to improve healthcare outcomes, I embarked on my data analysis journey. Over the past several years, I've honed my skills through projects, Google's Advanced Data Analytics Certification, LinkedIn Learning courses, and Coursera programs. I eagerly sought out opportunities to take on projects to put these skills into action both in my healthcare roles and elsewhere.

My unique set of skills allow me to uncover hidden insights with the perfect blend of science and technology. My degree in Behavioral Neuroscience equips me to understand human behavior, while technical skills in SQL, Python, and Excel allow me to wrangle and analyze data. Data visualization tools like Power BI and Tableau bring my findings to life with stunning clarity. This powerful combo makes me a data analyst who can not only crunch numbers but also tell compelling stories that drive results.

My analytical skills are constantly evolving, and I'm eager to contribute to a team that values data-driven decision making. Explore my projects to see how I can translate data into actionable insights.
